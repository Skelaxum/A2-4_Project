Outcome


If ‘MediPlanner’ were to be successful it would mean many positive things for Australia and its residents. 'MediPlanner' would provide a highly optimized medical system allowing for a more straight-forward approach to healthcare for all involved . ‘Mediplanner’ would help revolutionize how Australians see healthcare, and no longer will the consensus be that it is a burden.

For patients this means faster and more smooth healthcare specific to the users preferences; for medical practitioners this means ease of access across all relevant fields unlike ever before and the ability to work in an up to date environment; and for the medical sector this means replacing outdated data reference and booking technology with an in-date alternative: 'MediPlanner'. Furthermore, 'MediPlanner' will allow for peace of mind for worrying parents in an ever growing world of mental health diagnosis.
