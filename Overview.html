Overview


MediPlanner is an application for mobile devices that will allow Doctors to find openings within Hospitals around Australia, it will allow Patients to find Doctors and for the Doctors to see the Patients information, and the application will have an early detection for mental health illnesses. A Doctor can download this application and register to find Hospitals all over Australia that has spots available for the Doctors required work. It will have options that the Doctor can choose to narrow down the types of Hospitals or medical centres that they can work at. Doctors can find information regarding patients that they have assigned to them, this would include a brief medical history, allergies, previous appointments and other relevant information. An early detection of mental health issues will be a part of the application and it will monitor behaviours and patterns in both patients and Doctors to find the early signs. This would allow the patient to learn more about mental illness and to alert a member of family of the early sign.

Motivation


One section of the application will focus on mental health protection for young adolescence aged between the ages (13-17). The motivation to add this section into the application is the devastating statistics that continue to rise regarding mental health disorders, self-harm and suicide within that age group. Since 2014 Suicide rates in this age group have skyrocketed. Adolescents having the highest prevalence of mental health issues out of all age groups. (Adolescent mental health, 2020) Additionally the adaption of social media coupled with accessibility to the internet is believed to be a main detriment to mental health. (Mental Health of Australian Young people and Adolecence, 2020)

Cyber bullying is at an all-time high, with 1 in 2 teenagers admitting to being bullied over the internet. (Donegan, 2012) Furthermore no one has had the accessibility to find ways to harm self until now.

Adolescence have the resources to harm themselves in sneaky and creative ways with guardians being gaslighted when it is to late. (Dog, 2018)

The main motivation for the practitioner element of the application is to save Hospitals money by cutting out recruitment agency fees. This application would save Hospitals millions every year.   Another area which motivated us for the creation of this application was accessibility for Hospitals/ Medical practitioners being able to share information easily. This will save Doctors/ Hospitals and other health professionals a considerable amount of time.   Medical professional would when required quickly be able to access patients records not only within Australia but universally, instead of having to wait for x rays to be emailed/ faxed over. Health professionals would be able to investigate prescription history and any other important details what they would need to accurately perform in their role. This would avoid miscommunication and patients performing unnecessary tests. This application also allows Hospitals/ GP clinics and other medical officers to communicate with one another more effectively.
